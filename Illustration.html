Dissertation Project

This project entailed writing and illustrating a childrens book titled "I got Lost". I created two versions of this book. Apart from the coloured book, I designed a braille version for Visually Impaired children. To provide these individuals with an art related experience, I embossed the illustrations and used a vareity of fabrics so that they would be able to "visualize" the narrative.
